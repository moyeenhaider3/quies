Privacy Policy

Quies — Mood Quotes & Music
Last updated: February 16, 2026

Introduction

Quies ("we", "our", "the app") is a free, ad-supported mobile application developed by Moyeen Haider. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your choices regarding your data.

By using Quies, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Quies stores the following data locally on your device using on-device storage (Hive). This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server:

  • Your mood selections and preferences
  • Bookmarked quotes
  • Theme and display settings
  • Onboarding progress
  • Music playback preferences

We do not have access to this data. It exists only on your device and is removed when you uninstall the app or clear app data.

Data Collected by Third-Party Services

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements within the app. AdMob may collect and process certain data automatically, including:

  • Device advertising identifier (Advertising ID / IDFA)
  • IP address (used for approximate geographic ad targeting)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, views)
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size)

This data is collected and processed by Google according to their policies. For details, see Google's Privacy Policy and How Google uses data from apps that use their services.

No Account Required

Quies does not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.

No Analytics

Quies does not use analytics services such as Firebase Analytics, Google Analytics, Mixpanel, or any similar tracking tools. We do not track how you use the app beyond the standard ad interaction data collected by AdMob.

Advertising

Quies is a free app that displays advertisements through Google AdMob. These ads help support the development and maintenance of the app.

In the European Economic Area (EEA) and other regions where required by law, a consent dialog is presented when you first use the app, allowing you to choose whether to receive personalized or non-personalized advertisements.

Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • All network communications (API requests, ad serving) use HTTPS encryption
  • Local data is stored on your device and protected by your device's built-in security features
  • We do not operate servers that store your personal data

Children's Privacy

Quies is rated for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Your Rights

For Users in the European Economic Area (GDPR)

If you are located in the EEA, you have the following rights:

  • Consent management: You can manage your ad personalization preferences through the consent dialog shown in the app
  • Data deletion: You can delete all locally stored data by clearing app data in your device's Settings, or by uninstalling the app
  • Opt out of personalized ads: You can opt out via the consent dialog or by resetting your device's advertising ID
  • Contact us: You may contact us regarding any data rights concerns

For Users in California (CCPA)

California residents have the right to know what data is collected and to request deletion. As Quies stores data only on your device, you can exercise these rights by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.

Data Retention

Local data (preferences, bookmarks) is retained on your device until you clear app data or uninstall Quies. We do not retain any data on external servers.

Data collected by Google AdMob is retained according to Google's data retention policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
